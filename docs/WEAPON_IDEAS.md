# Weapon Ideas

Creative new weapon concepts for Superfighters Redux, organized by category. These focus on filling gaps in the current roster — particularly **crowd control**, **mobility tools**, **trick shots**, and **area denial**.

---

## Melee

### Harpoon
Long reach, slow thrust. On hit, pulls the target toward you (like a hook). High risk/reward positioning tool.

### Electro Gauntlet
Very short range melee (fist-mounted). Chance to stun on hit, briefly disabling opponent movement. Low damage but high utility.

### Boomerang Axe
Melee that can be thrown (alt-fire). Returns to the thrower after a short arc; deals damage on the way out *and* back. Lost if not caught.

### Nunchaku
Fast combo weapon with very short range. Each consecutive hit in a combo adds damage. Resets if you miss.

---

## Handguns

### Ricochet Pistol
Fires a single bullet that bounces off walls/floors up to 3 times. Low direct damage but excels in tight corridors and around cover.

### Grapple Gun
Zero damage. Fires a hook that pulls you to the impact point (wall, ceiling, player). Cooldown-based. Huge mobility tool.

### Sawed-Off Flare Launcher
Short-range handgun that fires an incendiary flare. Sets players/environment on fire. Low ammo, single shot.

---

## Rifles

### Tesla Rifle
Continuous electric beam (like a short-range Lazer). Arcs to a second nearby enemy for reduced damage. Drains ammo fast.

### Harpoon Gun
Fires a bolt that pins enemies to walls/objects. Target is immobilized for a short time. One shot, slow reload.

### Rivet Gun
Semi-auto, medium damage, but projectiles embed in surfaces. Hitting a surface near an enemy causes a small shrapnel burst after a delay.

### Ice Cannon
Fires a frost projectile that slows enemies on hit, stacking to a full freeze. Low damage, support/crowd-control oriented.

---

## Thrown

### Decoy Grenade
Creates a fake player clone that runs forward for a few seconds, drawing enemy fire/AI attention. Explodes weakly at the end.

### Bola
Thrown weapon that entangles a target's legs, tripping them and slowing movement for a duration. No damage, pure crowd control.

### Smoke Bomb
Creates a large obscuring cloud. Players inside are hidden from others outside. No damage, tactical repositioning tool.

### Molten Flask
Creates a lingering pool of fire on impact (like a Molotov but smaller and longer-lasting). Area denial for chokepoints.

### Gravity Bomb
After a delay, pulls all nearby players/objects toward the epicenter for 2 seconds, then releases. Pairs well with other throwables.

---

## Pickups / Utility

### Berserk Potion
Doubles melee damage and attack speed for 8 seconds, but you can't use ranged weapons during the effect.

### Cloak Device
Grants temporary invisibility (disrupted if you attack). Quick in-and-out flanking tool.

### Magnetic Boots
Walk on walls and ceilings for a limited time. Unique mobility option different from jetpack.
